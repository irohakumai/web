Hanoi

Introducing Hanoi, the city where I study abroad. And what I will learn.

Hanoi

Hanoi is the capital of Vietnam and the center of politics and culture. Hoan Kiem Lake, the Old Town and the water puppet show are popular with tourists. Halong Bay is near Hanoi. Water puppet show is a traditional performing art that has been loved by Vietnamese people for about 1000 years.

University to study abroad

The university I study abroad at is the National University of Vietnam, Hanoi University of Social Sciences and Humanities. This university has a good reputation in Vietnamese language education and has produced many excellent human resources such as ambassadors from each country. there are many partner universities for overseas, and we can interact with international students from all over the world.
