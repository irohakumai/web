to Vietnam

I am a Japanese university student.In September 2022, I will go to Vietnam to study abroad for about 10 months.
So, I will introduce the Vietnamese culture and life that I learned.

Basic Data

The capital is Hanoi. The official language is Vietnamese. The regime is a socialist republic. The population is about 96 million.

Culture

Vietnam starts early in the morning.Business time is from 8 o'clock, which is earlier than in Japan. In addition, some restaurants are open from 6 o'clock.

Food

The staple food in Vietnam is rice. Vietnam has a long north-south terrain, so there are different food cultures in the north, central, and south.

World Heritage

Vietnam has 7 World Heritage Sites.Famous ones are Hoi An and Halong Bay.Hoi An is a nostalgic city with historic buildings.Various lanterns light up at night.We can see a mysterious and magnificent view of 2000 strange rocks of various sizes.

Vietnamese food culture

Vietnam's climate varies greatly depending on the region, so there are different food cultures in the north, central, and south.Since the northern part is adjacent to China, it is refreshing with salt and soy souse as the base seasoning.The central part is characterized by spicy dishes using spices such as capsium.The south part is characterized by a sweet flavor using coconut milk.
