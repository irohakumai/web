Capital

The capital is Hanoi. Hanoi is a city located in the northern part of Vietnam. It is said to be the city of politics and culture. And it is a city with a long history of 1000 years.

Language

The official language of Vietnam is Vietnamese. Vietnamese uses the alphabet and is written with "tone symbols" and "vowel symbols" above and below it. Due to the influence of French colonial rule, some words have become established as loanwords from French. And some areas have dialects.

Ethnic composition

Vietnam is a multi-ethnic country consisting of various ethnic groups. The ethnic composition of Vietnam is about 86% of the ethnic group called Kin tribe, and the remaining proportion is 53 minorities.

Weather

Since Vietnam is long from north to south, the climate varies from region to region. Northen Vietnam has a subtropical climate, so there are four seasons. The southern part has a tropical monsoon climate, so it is divided into a dry season and a rainy season.

Vietnam's famous food

Vietnam has many famous and delicious foods. I will introduse "Pho". Pho is a national dish of Vietnam. Pho is a kishimen-shaped flat noodle made by kneading rice flour and water. And, there are differences in how to make and season depending on the region. In the north, it is simple pho made by adding salt to a beef-based soup and topped with condiment green onions. In the southern, a soup with a strong sweetness and spices, and is a pho with various ingredients.

Vietnam's famous World Heritage Site

Vietnam has eight World Heritage Sites. I will introduse Hoi An one of the World Heritage Sites. Hoi An is one of the most famous tourist destinations in Vietnam. There is 1 hour 20 minutes by plane from Hanoi. It is a nostalgic city with historic buildings. At night, colorful lanterns light up.
